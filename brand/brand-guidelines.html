Brand Identity System

Paraíso de Aves

This document defines the visual language of Paraíso de Aves across every touchpoint — from the browser favicon to official certification documents. Follow these guidelines to ensure the brand reads as one unified, premium experience.

01 — Brand Identity

The Mark

The Paraíso de Aves mark is built around three macaw tail feathers — the most distinctive visual signature of the Blue & Gold Macaw, the species most associated with the brand. The feathers fan outward from a single point, evoking both nature and prestige. Above them, the signature gold arc references the rainbow motif of the illustrated primary logo, creating visual continuity between the full identity and the geometric mark.

Full Emblem

brand/emblem.svg — Used in seals, certificates, the loading animation, and any premium context at 80px+

Icon Mark / Favicon

favicon.svg — Simplified for browser tabs, app icons, and any context under 80px

Watermark

brand/watermark.svg — White outline version for photo overlays. Apply at 15–25% opacity.

Clear Space Rule

Always maintain clear space equal to ½ × the mark diameter on all sides. Nothing — typography, imagery, or other graphic elements — should intrude into this protected zone.

Minimum size: 24px diameter on screen · 8mm on print. Below this, use only the primary illustrated logo.

10 — Photography

Photography Style

Subject & Composition

  • Birds as the clear subject — sharp, well-lit, expressive
  • Natural perch or hand-held contexts preferred
  • Complementary backgrounds: bokeh greens, cream walls, or clean studio dark
  • No filters or heavy post-processing — authentic colour
  • Eye-level or slightly below — never looking down at the bird

Tone & Treatment

  • Warm colour temperature (5500–6000K) preferred
  • High contrast between subject and background
  • Feather detail is the priority — never sacrifice sharpness for DOF
  • Watermark all original photography using .pa-photo-wrap or the SVG overlay
  • No stock images that compete with original photography

11 — Accessibility

Accessibility Standards

Colour Contrast

  • White on Forest Green (#1B3D2F): 11.5:1 ✓ AAA
  • Gold (#C9A24B) on Deep Forest: 7.8:1 ✓ AAA
  • Gold (#C9A24B) on White: 2.9:1 — for large text only
  • Dark text (#1A2A1E) on Cream: 14.2:1 ✓ AAA

Implementation Notes

  • All SVG brand assets include role="img" and aria-label
  • All <img> referencing brand assets must include descriptive alt text
  • Focus rings: outline:2px solid #C9A24B; offset:3px
  • Minimum touch target: 44×44px for all interactive elements
  • The loading animation respects prefers-reduced-motion

12 — International

Global Identity

Paraíso de Aves operates across ES, PT, FR, and EN markets. The visual identity is language-agnostic — the mark, colours, and typography translate unchanged into every market. Only text content, taglines, and seal labels are localised.
