Legal Framework for Buying Exotic Birds in Europe

The European Union regulates the trade of exotic parrots under Council Regulation (EC) No 338/97, which implements CITES within the EU. Most parrot species available through ParaisoDeAves are listed under CITES Appendix II or are captive-bred exemptions under Appendix I.

Key points for buyers:

  • All Annex A species (e.g., Hyacinth Macaw, certain cockatoos) require an EU Annex A certificate for sale within the EU
  • Captive-bred Annex B species (e.g., African Grey, most macaws, Amazon parrots) require a CITES certificate or breeding declaration
  • Import into non-EU countries (e.g., Switzerland, UK) requires additional import permits — we advise on this
  • We provide complete documentation packs to make compliance straightforward for all buyers
