## Rule
All gallery pages are generated by `generate-phase6d.js`. Do not hand-edit category pages — regenerate from the script instead.

**Why:** The image database (IMAGES[]) and category metadata (CATS[]) in the script are the single source of truth. Editing HTML directly would get overwritten on next generation.

## Structure
- `galeria/index.html` — 9-card category landing (ES)
- `fr/galerie/index.html` — 9-card category landing (FR)
- `galeria/{slug}/index.html` × 9 — ES category pages
- `fr/galerie/{slug}/index.html` × 9 — FR category pages
