Why Paraíso de Aves?

We are not a pet shop. We are a registered specialist breeding facility — one of Spain's most experienced psittacine breeders.

👐

Hand-Raised from Day One

Every bird is fed by human hands from the first days of life, socialised with people, and prepared for companion life before leaving our care.

📋

Complete CITES Documentation

Every sale includes full CITES certificates, veterinary health certificate, and individual ring identification. Complete legal compliance, always.

✈️

Specialist UK Delivery

We have years of experience delivering live parrots to the UK and Ireland via specialist live-animal air cargo. We manage every step of the process.

🏆

Registered Zoological Nucleus

We operate under official zoological nucleus registration in Valencia, Spain — the legal framework for commercial psittacine breeding.

🩺

Veterinary Certification

Every bird leaves with a veterinary health certificate issued within 10 days of travel. Our avian vet team monitors each bird from birth.

💬

Ongoing Support

We remain available to answer questions throughout the acclimatisation period and beyond. You are never alone with a new bird.

UK & Ireland Delivery

Delivered Safely to Your Nearest Airport

We deliver parrots throughout the United Kingdom — London, Manchester, Birmingham, Glasgow, Edinburgh, Bristol, and beyond — and to Ireland, including Dublin, Cork, and Galway.

Birds travel via specialist live-animal air cargo in IATA-approved crates, with food, water, and temperature-controlled conditions throughout. We manage all CITES documentation, veterinary certificates, and customs clearance.
