Visiting Our Facility

We welcome prospective owners to visit our aviary in Llíria, Valencia. Meeting our birds in person is the best possible introduction to your future companion. Please contact us by email to arrange a visit — we accommodate visitors throughout the year.

Llíria is approximately 30 minutes by road from Valencia city centre and served by regular train services from Valencia Joaquín Sorolla station.
