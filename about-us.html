About ParaisoDeAves

Spain's trusted exotic parrot breeder — raising extraordinary birds and connecting them with extraordinary people across Spain and Europe since our founding.

Our Story

ParaisoDeAves — known in Spanish as Paraíso de Loros (Parrot Paradise) — was born from a lifelong passion for exotic birds. Our journey began long before it became a business. Growing up surrounded by birds, our founders developed a deep respect for the intelligence, sensitivity and complexity of parrot species that most people never get to witness up close.

Over time, what started as personal enthusiasm grew into professional breeding. We built purpose-designed aviaries where different species could thrive in conditions that replicate, as closely as possible, their natural environments. We studied nutrition, behaviour, socialization techniques, avian medicine, and the legal frameworks that govern responsible bird breeding across Spain and Europe.

Today, ParaisoDeAves is recognized as one of Spain's most trusted sources for hand-raised exotic parrots. We serve buyers across Spain — from Madrid and Barcelona to Valencia, Seville and beyond — and ship regularly to France, Germany, Portugal, Italy, the Netherlands and other EU countries. Every bird we place carries our reputation, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Our Breeding Philosophy

We believe that a parrot is not a product. Each bird is a sentient being with emotional needs, social requirements and a lifespan that will outlast most domestic pets. This belief underpins everything we do:

  • No overcrowding: Our aviaries maintain low densities so every bird receives adequate space, light, and environmental enrichment.
  • Species-appropriate nutrition: We invest heavily in high-quality, species-specific diets rather than the cheapest available feed.
  • Socialization protocols: Chicks are handled from day one using careful, age-appropriate techniques that build trust without stress.
  • Pair matching: Breeding pairs are selected based on genetic compatibility, health records, and behavioural compatibility.
  • Continuous learning: Our team stays current with the latest avian research, attending specialist conferences and maintaining relationships with leading avian veterinarians.

Our Commitment to Buyers

Buying an exotic parrot is a major decision — emotionally, financially, and logistically. We treat every inquiry with the seriousness it deserves. We will never pressure you into a purchase. We will take the time to understand your lifestyle, your experience level, and your expectations so that we can recommend the species that is genuinely right for you.

If you are a first-time parrot owner, we may recommend starting with a more manageable species before moving to a large macaw or cockatoo. Our goal is always a successful, lifelong match between bird and owner — not just a completed sale.
