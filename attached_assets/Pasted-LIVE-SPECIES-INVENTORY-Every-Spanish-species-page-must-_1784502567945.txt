DYNAMIC INVENTORY

When a new bird is added to the CMS:

- Automatically create its profile page.
- Automatically add it to its species page.
- Automatically update the available bird count.
- Automatically update the sitemap.
- Automatically update internal links.

When a bird is marked as sold:

- Display a "Vendido" badge.
- Keep the profile indexed unless manually removed.
- Recommend similar available birds.
