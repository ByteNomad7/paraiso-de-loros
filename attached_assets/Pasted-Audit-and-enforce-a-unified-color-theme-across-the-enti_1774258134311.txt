Audit and enforce a unified color theme across the entire website https://paraisodeaves.com.

GOAL:
Ensure the website uses a consistent premium color system (green, gold, cream) with no random or mismatched colors.

---

1. FULL COLOR AUDIT
- Scan all pages, components, and sections:
  - header
  - footer
  - buttons
  - links
  - forms
  - cards
  - backgrounds
  - hover states

- Detect any colors that do not match the brand palette

---

2. APPROVED COLOR SYSTEM

Apply only these colors:

- Primary (green): #1F3D2B
- Secondary green: #2B533C
- Accent (gold): #D4A94F
- Accent hover: #B8933E
- Background (cream): #F8F5F0
- Surface (white): #FFFFFF
- Text (dark): #1A1A1A
- Secondary text: #5C5C5C
- Border: #E7E0D2

Optional (limited use):
- Highlight red: #E63946

---

3. REMOVE INCONSISTENT COLORS
- Remove or replace:
  - purple
  - blue
  - random greys
  - neon or overly bright tones

Replace them with the approved palette.

---

4. BUTTON CONSISTENCY
- All primary buttons must use:
  - background: #D4A94F
  - hover: #B8933E
- No mixed button styles across pages

---

5. LINK COLORS
- Standard links:
  - color: #1F3D2B
- Hover:
  - color: #D4A94F

Ensure no purple/blue default links remain.

---

6. BACKGROUND CONSISTENCY
- Main pages: #F8F5F0
- Cards/forms: #FFFFFF
- Header/Footer: #1F3D2B

Avoid mixing dark and light themes inconsistently.

---

7. FORM & INPUTS
- Background: white
- Border: #E7E0D2
- Focus: gold highlight

---

8. HOVER & INTERACTIONS
- Ensure all hover effects follow:
  - gold accent or subtle darkening
- No random hover colors

---

9. GLOBAL CSS VARIABLES (IMPORTANT)
If not already used, create:

:root {
  --primary: #1F3D2B;
  --accent: #D4A94F;
  --bg: #F8F5F0;
  --text: #1A1A1A;
}

Ensure all colors are pulled from variables, not hardcoded randomly.

---

10. FINAL CHECK
- No mixed themes (dark + light conflicts)
- No leftover default browser colors
- No inconsistent button styles
- Visual consistency across all pages

---

DO NOT:
- Change content or layout
- Only fix color consistency and styling

---

FINAL RESULT:
A clean, unified, premium visual identity using a consistent green/gold/cream palette across the entire site.
