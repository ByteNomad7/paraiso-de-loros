====================================================
DESIGN PRINCIPLES
====================================================

The brand should feel like:

• Rolex
• Four Seasons
• National Geographic
• Apple

Avoid anything that feels:

• generic
• template-based
• cartoonish
• clipart
• AI-generated
• overly decorative

====================================================
PHOTO BRANDING
====================================================

Create a subtle watermark system for all bird photography.

The watermark should use only the new brand emblem.

It must be elegant, semi-transparent and suitable for protecting original photography without distracting from the bird.
