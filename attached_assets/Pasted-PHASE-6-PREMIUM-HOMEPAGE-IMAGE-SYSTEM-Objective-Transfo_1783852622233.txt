PHASE 6 — PREMIUM HOMEPAGE IMAGE SYSTEM

Objective:
Transform the homepage of Paraíso de Aves into a premium visual experience using a curated collection of our very best real photographs.

IMPORTANT

Do not use random images.

Create a dedicated homepage image library:

/public/images/homepage/

This folder will contain only the highest quality photographs that represent the Paraíso de Aves brand.

=========================================
IMAGE MANAGEMENT
=========================================

Use only premium images for the homepage.

Recommended number:

15–25 carefully selected photographs.

These should include:

• Facility exterior
• Beautiful aviaries
• Hand-feeding
• Socialised parrots
• Hyacinth Macaw
• Blue-and-Yellow Macaw
• Scarlet Macaw
• African Grey
• Cockatoo
• Amazon Parrot
• Conure
• Toucan
• Premium cages
• Healthy food preparation
• Professional transport carrier
• Bird interacting with handler

Do not use blurry photos.

Do not stretch images.

Crop intelligently while preserving the bird.
