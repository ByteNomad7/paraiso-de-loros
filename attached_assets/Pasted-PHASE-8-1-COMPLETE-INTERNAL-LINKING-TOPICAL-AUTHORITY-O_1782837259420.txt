Objective:
Perform a complete internal linking optimization across the entire Paraíso de Aves website to strengthen topical authority, improve crawlability, distribute PageRank, and increase rankings in Spain, Portugal, and France.

IMPORTANT

• Do NOT change existing URLs.
• Do NOT remove pages.
• Do NOT break navigation.
• Preserve all existing SEO metadata.
• Keep the premium design.
• All new internal links must feel natural for users.
• Prioritize contextual links over navigation links.
