PHASE 6D — RESTRUCTURE GALLERY BY CATEGORY

Problem:
The current Spanish and French gallery pages display mixed images randomly in one masonry grid. This looks disorganized and does not help visitors understand the facility, birds, food, cages, or care standards.

Goal:
Rebuild the gallery into clearly separated visual categories with short explanatory text, proper headings, and category-specific galleries.

IMPORTANT

- Do not display all images in one mixed gallery.
- Do not mix bird species, food, cages and facility photos together.
- Keep the premium green and gold design.
- Preserve lightbox, lazy loading and responsive image optimization.
- Use the same real photos, but organize them correctly.
- Spanish and French must have equivalent structure with localized text.
- Do not show an empty category.
- Do not mislabel any image.

========================================
1. GALLERY LANDING PAGE
========================================

Spanish:
/galeria

French:
/fr/galerie

Replace the current random masonry wall with a category landing page.

Create premium category cards:

Spanish:

- Alimentación y nutrición
- Jaulas y aviarios
- Guacamayos
- Conuros
- Tucanes
- Loros disponibles
- Crianza y socialización
- Transporte y entrega

- Alimentation et nutrition
- Cages et volières
- Aras
- Conures
- Toucans
- Perroquets disponibles
- Élevage et socialisation
- Transport et livraison

Each category card must contain:

- one strong featured photo
- category title
- short description of 30–60 words
- actual number of photos in the category
- localized CTA:
  Spanish: Ver fotos
  French: Voir les photos
- link to the dedicated category section/page

Do not show more than 9 category cards on the main gallery landing page.

========================================
3. CATEGORY INTRODUCTION TEXT
========================================

Write concise, natural text.

Example Spanish — Instalaciones:

"Conozca los espacios donde cuidamos, alimentamos y socializamos a nuestras aves. Esta selección muestra nuestras zonas de crianza, aviarios, áreas de descanso y preparación diaria."

Example French — Installations:

"Découvrez les espaces dans lesquels nos oiseaux sont élevés, nourris et socialisés. Cette sélection présente nos zones d’élevage, volières, espaces de repos et installations de préparation quotidienne."

Food category should explain:
- fresh foods
- fruit and vegetables
- seed mixes
- feeding preparation
- species-specific nutrition

Bird categories should explain:
- species shown
- temperament
- socialisation
- care
- availability disclaimer

/public/images/nursery/
/public/images/socialization/
→ crianza-socializacion / elevage-socialisation

/public/images/transport/
→ transporte / transport

Do not use AI visual recognition as the primary classification method when the folder already identifies the category.

Do not place:
- food photos inside bird galleries
- toucans inside macaw galleries
- cages inside facility unless clearly relevant
- stock photography alongside real facility photography without labeling

========================================
5. GALLERY LAYOUT
========================================

On category pages:

- show 12–18 photos initially
- add "Ver todas las fotos" / "Voir toutes les photos"
- expand or load more progressively
- keep masonry layout only inside each category
- preserve consistent spacing
- use clean captions
- avoid oversized random image heights
- use stable card aspect ratios where possible
- no chaotic visual ordering

Order images intelligently:

1. strongest hero image
2. overview photos
3. detail photos
4. close-ups
5. supporting images
